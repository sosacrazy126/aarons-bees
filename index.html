🔧 What We Do

Services That Protect You — And the Bees

From emergency swarm removal to long-term prevention, Aaron's team handles it all with care and professionalism.

Live Bee Removal

Safe extraction and relocation of honey bee colonies from walls, roofs, trees, and structures. We never kill bees — we relocate them.

Bed Bug Heat Treatment

Reinfestation Prevention

📋 How It Works

From Your Call to Problem Solved

We keep it simple and transparent. Here's what to expect when you reach out.

1

You Call — We Listen

Tell us what's going on. How long have the bees been there? How high up? Anyone allergic? We'll ask the right questions to understand your situation.

2

On-Site Assessment

Aaron comes out to evaluate in person. $175 service call covers the assessment — and that fee goes directly toward your removal if you move forward.

Using eco-friendly, non-lethal methods, we carefully extract the colony and relocate them where they can thrive. No chemicals, no killing.

Repair & Prevention
